What We Offer

My website offers users the opportunity to watch and learn card tricks. It gives people the ability, and it is optional, to learn the trick via a tutorial. This funcitonlity was added as many people like to keep the WOW factor of watching a card trick and not knowing how it was done. There is also the ability to contact me through the contact form with any feedback or questions.

The Inspiration Of My Website

There are two inspirations which made me want to create this website. The first, my brother. I do not see much of my brother and we have very little in common, however, card tricks is one thing we can bond over. With the lack of seeing him and the added problem of COVID-19, my brother and me were unable to really talk or bond over anything. So I had the idea of creating a website where he can still learn tricks from me but also so can everyone else who wishes to. The second, my grandpa. He suffers from vascular dementia and before he was diagnosed he loved card tricks. He would always perform them for his grandchildren, his children and my nan but he does not have that luxury anymore. At first I carried the tricks on in person with him but now he is at a point where he cannot comprehend what is going on or who I am and I saw this frustrated him. So this is a way for him to not stress about who is performing the trick but just to be entertained by it.
